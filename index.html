Research Interests

I work on designing and analyzing algorithmic systems to address social challenges such as fairness and online governance.. Broadly, my research interests lie at the intersection of algorithm design and social computing. If you'd like to learn more about my research, feel free to reach out via email or Twitter!

My Theoretical CS (TCS) work (advised by Prof. Chandra Chekuri) centers around

  • Approximation Algorithms and Optimization
  • Fairness in Algorithm Design, Fair Clustering, and Facility Location problems

My Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) work (advised by Prof. Eshwar Chandrasekharan, the PI of SCUBA LAB) focuses on

  • Social Computing, Data Science, and Human-Centered AI
  • Content Moderation on Text- and Audio- Based Platforms
